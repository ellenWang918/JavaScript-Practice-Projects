✨ Welcome to the dice rolling game!

To start the game, simply click 🎲 Roll dice

Player 1 will go first, and their score will be added up as they keep rolling the dice. If a Player rolls a 1, they will lose all their current score and it will be next Player's turn.

To keep the score, simply click the 📥 Hold

The game ends when one player reaches a score of 💯 100. They will be declared the winner!

If you want to start a new game, just click 🔄 New game

Have fun playing!
